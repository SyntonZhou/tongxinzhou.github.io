This work proposes **HYPER-SK**, a hardware accelerator tailored for **manifold-constrained hyper-connections (mHC)**. It redesigns key computation and communication components to better match hardware execution, aiming for substantially improved throughput and efficiency while preserving comparable numerical accuracy.
